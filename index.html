What Percentage of COVID Tests are Positive in Indian States?

Before reopening a region, the WHO recommends that the percentage of COVID tests that are positive should be less than 5 percent. This means that out of 100 tests conducted, fewer than 5 should be positive on average.

⚠️ The data reported on this page may not accurately reflect the ground reality due to a lack of testing in rural areas, testing hesitancy, a shortage of widespread test availability, and potential errors in data collection. Please be cautious in interpreting this data and remember that this is necessarily an incomplete picture, as it leaves out everyone who has not been tested or does not have access to tests.

Which Indian States Meet WHO Standards For Reopening?

For a region to reopen, the World Health Organization recommends the the percentage of new COVID tests that come back positive should be 5 percent or less. This means that out of every 100 tests conducted in the region, no more than 5 tests should be positive on average.
